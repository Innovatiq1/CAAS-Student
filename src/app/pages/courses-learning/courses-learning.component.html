Description

Welcome to the Complete Aviation Security and Safety Management (AVSEC), the only course you need to learn to rules and become a Safety & Security Officer. With 150,000+ ratings and a 4.8 average, my AVSEC course is one of the HIGHEST RATED courses in the history of CAAS!
We'll take you step-by-step through engaging video tutorials and teach you everything you need to know to succeed as a Safety & Security Officer.
Throughout this comprehensive course, we cover a massive amount of safety and security, including:
• Various Safety and security Groups and Components
• Access Controls
• Human Measures - Screening, Profiling and Threatening & Non Threatening Categories
• Various protection layers
• Security measures
• Procedures at the land side
• Airside/ Sterile areas
• Patrolling/ Challenges and Threats
• Dangerous Goods screening Baggage/ Passenger Screening
• Advanced Imaging Technology
• Larger X RAY Scanners - X RAY technologies to screen Cabin and Cargo Luggage Various other tools & Equipnment used for screening.

What you'll learn

  • Air Transport Safety and Security
  • Management - AVSEC - Intermediate (Regular)
